Discovery

During the discovery phase, we discuss what your needs and wants from your website are, how we can turn your website into an asset and validate all ideas. You can expect questions about the following on the discovery call:

  • Project timeline
  • Your business goals
  • The goal of your website (example: increase traffic, increase sales, brand awareness, etc.)
  • Website features
  • Who your target audience is
  • Industry trends
